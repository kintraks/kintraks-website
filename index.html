About Kintraks

Kintraks is an animal record system designed for breeders and animal enthusiasts. Enter the species and breed to customize it for any animal of your choice. Use advanced storage options and features to enhance and simplify your animal breeding and record-keeping.

Compatible with Windows, Mac, and Linux Desktop systems.
